About Me

Academic Excellence & Research Foundation

I graduated Summa Cum Laude with a BSc Honours in Biotechnology from the University of the Western Cape, where I developed a strong foundation in scientific research, laboratory techniques, and precision medicine. My academic journey was complemented by hands-on experience in DNA extraction, PCR analysis, and data collection in precision medicine research.

As a dedicated academic tutor for over four years, I honed my ability to communicate complex scientific concepts clearly and effectively, supporting diverse student groups in biotechnology and life sciences.

Technology & AI Expertise

My passion for technology led me to pursue extensive training in artificial intelligence and machine learning. I've completed comprehensive certifications from leading institutions including DeepLearning.AI, Stanford University, Microsoft, IBM, and Google Cloud, covering everything from supervised learning to generative AI and large language models.

Currently serving as an IT Support Candidate at CAPACITI, I provide technical support while continuously expanding my expertise in AI systems, cloud platforms, and emerging technologies that bridge the gap between biotechnology and digital innovation.

Community Impact & Leadership

Throughout my career, I've been committed to community service and public health initiatives. From serving as a Peer Educator in the UWC HIV/AIDS Unit to managing vaccination sites during the COVID-19 pandemic, I've consistently demonstrated my dedication to improving community health outcomes.

My leadership experience spans from being a school prefect and blood donor representative to coordinating patient flow in high-volume healthcare settings. I believe in using technology and scientific knowledge to create meaningful positive impact in communities.
